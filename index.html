About Us

Alamada, located in the province of Cotabato in the Philippines, is a town blessed with diverse landscapes that range from lush mountains to serene lakes. The town's name is derived from the Cebuano word "Alam" which means "to know" or "to be aware," a fitting name for a place that beckons travelers to discover its secrets.
